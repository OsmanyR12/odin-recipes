Baked Brie Recipe

This baked Brie recipe is simple to make with a wheel of brie cheese wrapped in golden puff pastry.

Ingredient (one person)

  • 1 sheet frozen puffed pastry. Thawed
  • 1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sliced almonds

Step-by-Step instructions

  1. Step 1: Gather ingredients. Preheat oven at 350 degrees F.
    Grease a 9-inch pie pan
  2. Step 2: Slice brie in half horizontally into flat wheels
  3. Step 3: Place pastry into the prepared pie pan.
    Place one wheel of brie with the rind facing down.
    sprinkel almonds evenly over Brie
  4. Step 4: Top with the remaining wheel, with the rind facing up
  5. Step 5: Bundle the puff pastry around the Brie. Pinch the ends together to seal
  6. Step 6: Put it in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
    Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving
