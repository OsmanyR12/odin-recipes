Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe will be easy, concise, and delicious

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Eggs
  • Cheese

Step by Step instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Step by Step: How To Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmessan cheese
  7. Repeat layer, then top it off with parmessan cheese

How Long Should You Bake It?

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
